Interesting Cats

Most people agree that cats are adorable creatures whether nice or mischievous. There are some that make you do a double take and not just because they are so cute. Here are some interesting cat breeds and the features that make them unsual.

Firstly, what are some features that can make a cat interesting?

  1. Fur
    • Lacking Fur
    • Textured Look
  2. Ears
    • Folded
    • Rounded
  3. Tail
    • Missing/Short
